# Product Guidelines\n\n## Prose Style\n- **Clear & Concise:** Use direct and helpful language, avoiding unnecessary jargon to ensure the information is easily understood by all users.\n- **Technically Accurate (Astronomical):** Maintain professional and accurate astronomical terminology when appropriate to cater to the target audience of amateur astronomers and DIY telescope makers.\n\n## UX Principles\n- **Intuitive Navigation:** Design the interface for ease of use, ensuring that common tasks like monitoring and control are intuitive and require minimal learning.\n\n## Visual Branding\n- **Night-Friendly Dark Theme:** Utilize a high-contrast dark theme to reduce glare and protect the user's night vision during astronomical observations.\n- **High Legibility:** Prioritize the legibility of labels, data displays, and controls, ensuring they are easily readable in low-light environments.\n- **Red-Light Compatibility Mode:** Incorporate a dedicated mode that uses red text or overlays to further minimize disruption to the user's night vision.\n
